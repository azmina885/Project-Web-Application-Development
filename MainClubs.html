Featured Clubs

Badminton Club

A club for students who can enjoy fast-paced ralies and teamwork. Open to all skill levels to join this club. It offers friendly matches, training sessions and many competitions ahead.

Futsal Club

A dynamic team that focuses on skillful, enjoyment during playing football. This club has active training activities and many tournaments that open for all members.

Netball Club

A dedicated club promoting teamwork and strategy through practice. Include many friendly games and inter-campus competitions.

Volleyball Club

The club for volleyball enthusiasts to improve their technical skills, fitness, and game strategy, competing in both internal and external campus tournaments.

 

Pencak Silat Club

A traditional martial arts club focused on building mental discipline, physical strength, and combat techniques, while upholding noble cultural values.

Ruhaniq Islamic Club

Dedicated to Islamic spiritual activities, spreading religious knowledge, in-depth studies, and community service based on Islamic values.

Persatuan Anak Borneo Club

A community uniting students from Kalimantan/Borneo, focusing on preserving local culture and strengthening kinship among students far from home.

Japanese Culture Club

The club for exploring and celebrating Japanese culture (language, cuisine, arts, and media) through interactive events and cultural exchange.

Debates Club

A club for students who want to sharpen critical thinking, rapid research, and public communication abilities through formal debate training and participation in various competitions.

Pembimbing & Kerjaya Club

This club offers personal development training, job skills workshops, and career information to prepare students for entering the professional world.

Accounting Club

An organization that deepens accounting knowledge beyond the classroom via case studies, software workshops, and preparation for professional certifications.

Entrepreneurship Club

A club for students interested in starting or developing a business, providing training, mentoring, and networking to foster an entrepreneurial spirit.

 

Vocalism Club

Focuses on developing individual and group vocal techniques (choir/vocal group), preparing members for high-quality performances at campus events.

Akasia Dance Club

A club for students interested in dance (modern, traditional, or contemporary) to learn choreography, develop personal style, and performing on stage.

Anak Teater Club

A performing arts club covering acting, directing, scriptwriting, and stage management to produce diverse plays and musicals.

Photography Club

This club for students to learn, share techniques, and sharpen their artistic eye in the world of photography. Active in host regular photo hunts, editing workshops, and exhibitions to showcase student work.
