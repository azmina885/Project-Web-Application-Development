Celebrate, connect, and create memories that last a lifetime!

ClubLink highlights the most exciting and meaningful events happening across the university every year. These signature events bring students together, showcase talent, and strengthen the spirit of our campus community. Whether you’re here to compete, perform, volunteer, or simply have fun — don’t miss these unforgettable moments!

Annual Club Carnival


The Club Carnival is one of the biggest highlights of the semester! It’s a fun and interactive event where all clubs and societies set up booths to showcase their activities and recruit new members. Students can explore different clubs, meet new friends, and sign up on the spot.

Activities may include:
• Club exhibitions and performances
• Game booths and lucky draws
• Registration counters for new members
• Live music and mini shows

 

Recognition Awards Night

The Recognition Awards Night celebrates the hard work and achievements of our clubs, societies, and student leaders. This event recognizes outstanding members who have made a positive impact through their dedication and leadership.

Awards Categories:
• Best Performing Club
• Most Active Member
• Community Service Award
• Event of the Year

 

Workshop & Seminar Series

The Workshop & Seminar Series provides valuable opportunities for students to learn new skills, gain inspiration, and network with professionals. Each session is hosted by clubs or invited speakers, focusing on leadership, personal development, and current trends.

Examples of topics:
• Leadership & Communication Skills
• Career Development
• Entrepreneurship & Innovation
• Mental Health Awareness
