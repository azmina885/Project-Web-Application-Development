Student Council

Oversees all registered clubs and societies. Responsible for approving new club applications, coordinating university-wide events, and representing student interests.

  • Mission & Authorities : The Student Council is the official governing body for the student population. Its mandate is to represent student interests and ensure a clear channel of communication with the university administration.
  • Key Responsibilities: Members are responsible for approving new student organizations and coordinating major university-wide events, promoting campus vitality and engagement.
  • Leadership Value: Participation offers significant experience in organizational governance and policy advocacy.

    Volunteer Group

    Focuses on social service and community engagement. Organizes volunteer programs, charity events, and outreach projects in collaboration with local and international partners.

  • Mission & Focus: This group is dedicated to fostering social responsibility through organized service and community outreach. We focus on creating a positive impact both locally and internationally.
  • Key Activities: We consistently organize structured volunteer programs and charity fundraising initiatives in partnership with external organizations.
  • Leadership Value: Volunteers gain essential skills in project planning, resource management, and ethical community engagement.
